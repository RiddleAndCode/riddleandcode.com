Product Overview

The key and identity management sets the following features on top of the R&C Security Technology and cloud services:
* Verification and Authentication
* Digital certificate management
* Password management
* Provisioning and de-provisioning of keys.

Key Features

Ease of Use: Simplified recovery processes leveraging mnemonic phrases (SLIP39, BIP39/44) for disaster recovery and backup.

Distributed key generation and sharing (SLIP39) enhances resilience against single points of failure.

Standardized key derivation paths (SLIP44, BIP44) simplify key management across different applications.

Benefits & Use Cases

Hardware wallet compatibility (SLIP21, BIP44) adds a layer of physical security for recovery keys.

Enhanced security posture and reduced risk of unauthorized access. Streamlined user onboarding and offboarding processes.

Improved operational efficiency and cost savings. Ensured regulatory compliance and auditability. Better user experience with simplified access to resources.

Use Cases

* User Authentication.
* Access to online services and apps.
* Participants of secure data exchange.

Core Capabilities

  • Digital Identity Management: Secure creation, storage, and verification of digital identities
  • Cryptographic Key Lifecycle: Complete key generation, distribution, rotation, and revocation
  • Multi-Factor Authentication: Hardware-backed authentication with biometric and password support
  • Certificate Authority Integration: PKI management with automated certificate provisioning
  • Standards Compliance: Support for BIP39/44, SLIP21/39/44 standards
  • Hardware Security Module: Integration with dedicated cryptographic hardware

Security Standards

  • BIP39/44: Hierarchical deterministic wallets with mnemonic seed phrases
  • SLIP21: Hierarchical derivation of symmetric keys
  • SLIP39: Shamir's Secret Sharing for mnemonic seed backup
  • SLIP44: Registered coin types for multi-currency support
  • X.509: Digital certificate standards for public key infrastructure
  • FIDO2/WebAuthn: Modern authentication protocols for web applications

Enterprise Benefits

  • Reduced Security Risk: Hardware-backed key storage eliminates software-based vulnerabilities
  • Operational Efficiency: Automated key lifecycle management reduces administrative overhead
  • Regulatory Compliance: Built-in audit trails and compliance reporting for industry standards
  • Scalability: Cloud-based management for thousands of devices and users
  • Cost Optimization: Reduced support costs through simplified recovery processes
  • Future-Proof: Support for emerging cryptographic standards and protocols
