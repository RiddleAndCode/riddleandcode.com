About Us

Mission

Founded in Vienna, Riddle&Code is committed to onboarding industries to Web3. The team's mission is to provide a trusted connection between the digital and physical worlds has enabled us to work with key industry partners, developing new technologies and solutions with an emphasis on sustainability.

As Riddle&Code enters a new era of Web3, we have evolved into a product-led service company and building on top of the RDDL Network. We want to shape the token economy and provide the best products and services to make this vision flourish.

Our Values

Innovation

We constantly push the boundaries of what's possible, developing cutting-edge solutions that bridge the physical and digital worlds through secure hardware and blockchain technology.

Sustainability

Environmental responsibility is at the core of our mission. We're committed to creating solutions that contribute to a more sustainable future through efficient energy management and carbon offset monetization.

Security

Trust is fundamental to everything we do. Our tamper-proof hardware and secure software solutions ensure the highest levels of security for industrial applications and digital assets.

Partnership

We believe in collaborative innovation. By working closely with industry partners, energy communities, and technology providers, we create solutions that deliver real-world value.
