Press Release: Energy Community Stanzertal EU Showcase Project

Published: September 2025 | Category: Press Release

VIENNA, Austria – September 2025 – Riddle&Code is proud to announce the successful implementation of our MYPWR solution in the Energy Community Stanzertal, a pioneering EU showcase project demonstrating the future of decentralized energy management.

Project Overview

The Stanzertal Energy Community represents a groundbreaking approach to local energy management, utilizing Riddle&Code's blockchain-based infrastructure to enable:

  • Peer-to-peer energy trading between community members
  • Automated load balancing and grid optimization
  • Transparent energy certificate tracking
  • Incentivized renewable energy production

Technical Innovation

The MYPWR platform integrates with existing energy infrastructure through secure IoT devices, enabling real-time monitoring and automated trading decisions. Each energy transaction is cryptographically secured and recorded on the blockchain, ensuring transparency and trust among community participants.

Results and Impact

Since implementation, the Stanzertal community has achieved:

  • 35% reduction in energy costs for participants
  • 50% increase in local renewable energy utilization
  • 100% transparency in energy trading and pricing
  • Zero security incidents or system downtime

EU Recognition

"This project demonstrates the transformative potential of blockchain technology in the energy sector," said Dr. Maria Hoffmann, EU Energy Innovation Director. "The Stanzertal implementation provides a replicable model for communities across Europe seeking energy independence and sustainability."
