About Riddle&Code
Bridging Industries to Web3

Founded in Vienna, Riddle&Code is committed to onboarding industries to Web3. Our mission is to provide a trusted connection between the digital and physical worlds, enabling us to work with key industry partners, developing new technologies and solutions with an emphasis on sustainability.

OUR MISSION

Shaping the Token Economy

Web3 Integration

We're building the infrastructure that enables traditional industries to seamlessly integrate with Web3 technologies. Our hardware-secured solutions provide the trust layer needed for real-world asset tokenization and decentralized operations.

Product-Led Innovation

As we enter a new era of Web3, we've evolved into a product-led service company building on top of the RDDL Network. We deliver complete solutions that combine hardware security with software platforms.

Sustainability Focus

Our emphasis on sustainability drives every solution we create. From enabling renewable energy communities to preventing supply chain fraud, we're committed to building technology that creates a better future.

Our Approach

Building trust through hardware-secured digital infrastructure

Hardware Security Foundation

Every solution starts with secure hardware that creates incorruptible digital identities. This foundation enables trustless interactions between machines, systems, and organizations.

Industry Partnerships

We collaborate with leading organizations across energy, manufacturing, finance, and supply chain sectors to develop practical Web3 solutions for real-world challenges.

Open Standards

We're committed to open protocols and standards-based approaches that ensure interoperability and prevent vendor lock-in. Our solutions integrate with existing infrastructure.

Continuous Innovation

From research to production, we maintain a culture of innovation. Our team constantly explores new applications of blockchain, IoT, and cryptographic technologies to solve emerging challenges.
