About Riddle&Code

Riddle&Code is a technology company specializing in verifiable proof of origin and asset certification across industries. The company drives the transformation towards trusted digital verification systems by enabling transparent, traceable, and sustainable production chains.
From renewable energy to industrial sectors like steel and wood, Riddle&Code creates the foundation for trusted digital markets where the authenticity and origin of products can be reliably traced throughout their lifecycle.
