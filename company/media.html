ABOUT US

Who We Are

Riddle&Code builds KERN — the infrastructure platform for distributed energy management. We serve utilities, aggregators and energy communities across Europe, providing autonomous asset connectivity, flexibility forecasting and dispatch.

Our technology, including MIOTY wireless connectivity and autonomous dispatch, is deployed in production with leading utilities in Austria — managing a live MV/LV microgrid, flexibility aggregator and energy community platform.
