Riddle&Code's Commitment to Sustainable Development

Riddle&Code is a company that places a strong commitment on sustainable development, recognizing the importance of addressing today's critical challenges to secure a better future for humanity. With an ever-growing population and finite resources, it is imperative to seek alternative business and technological practices to secure the planet for future generations. Riddle&Code aims to contribute to this goal by introducing necessary technology that can help the planet.

The company focuses on ensuring the integrity and authenticity of products throughout their entire lifecycle through Proof of Origin and Guarantee of Origin systems. By leveraging innovative blockchain technology, they enable these comprehensive origin verifications and product guarantees. The company aims to support 500 global enterprises in implementing these transparent verification systems by 2027. Their vision is an interconnected economy where the authenticity and origin of every product can be reliably traced. The company believes that this transparency and traceability are crucial for building sustainable and trustworthy supply chains, thus making an important contribution to solving current economic challenges.
