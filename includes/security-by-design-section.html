SECURITY BY DESIGN

Built-In Regulatory Compliance

Our security-first architecture isn't just about protecting data — it's designed from the ground up to meet the stringent requirements of modern regulatory frameworks. By embedding security at the hardware level, we enable organizations to achieve and maintain compliance across multiple jurisdictions.

GDPR Compliance

Hardware-level encryption and access control ensure data privacy by design and by default. Our architecture supports data minimization, secure deletion, and cryptographic proof of consent — core requirements of the General Data Protection Regulation.

NIS2 Directive

Meet Network and Information Security Directive requirements with hardware-attested security measures. Our technology provides secure communication and supply chain security necessary for critical infrastructure protection.

Cyber Resilience Act (CRA)

Future-proof your products with security that meets the EU Cyber Resilience Act. Hardware-based secure boot and verified updates ensure compliance throughout the product lifecycle.

Data Act Compliance

Enable fair data access and portability while maintaining security and control. Our cryptographic identity system supports selective disclosure and verifiable data sharing — key requirements of the EU Data Act.
